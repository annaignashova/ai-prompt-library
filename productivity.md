# Productivity Prompts

## Break down a complex task

Prompt:

Act as an experienced project coordinator.

Break the following task into small actionable steps.

For each step provide:
- objective
- estimated effort
- possible risks
- expected outcome

---

## Prioritize tasks

Prompt:

Help me prioritize the following tasks.

Rank them by:
- urgency
- impact
- effort

Explain your reasoning and suggest the best order of execution.

---

## Daily planning

Prompt:

Create a structured plan for today based on my priorities.

Include:
- deep work
- quick wins
- breaks
- review

Provide a realistic and balanced schedule.
